iConcept Accelerator Program

Introduction

Accelerator Program is a full-stack discovery program designed to help agribusinesses transform their business processes. It begins with a critical assessment of capabilities, constraints and milestones to uncover your team's priorities, use cases and desired outcomes. This assessment and diagnostic helps to establish a measurement framework that fuels the development of your agribusiness intelligence dashboards.

The process

1) Frame the biggest challenges faced by agribusinesses through constraints in the value chain. What are the key constraints coming in the way of achieving your strategic goals?

2) Map the Big Picture - Map the entire agribusiness value chain onto the white board. The body of value chain activities map is broken down to small sub-activities - user activies which support the value chain.

3) Sketch the User Personas using your agribusiness application. Sketching user personas addresses the following questions a) Who is your user? What do you know about what they do and how they behave? b) What is your user's goal? What do they need to do ? c) As it stands, what problem prevents them from meeting their goal?

4) Map Detailed Workflows with pre-conditions, post-conditions and exception scenarios.

5) Slice Out Development Strategy Slice the first release of your map into two or more delivery phases that allow you and your team to learn fast and avoid risk.
